# Campaign Marketplace Platform

> A three-sided marketplace where business owners post campaign briefs, niche knowledge persons pitch and assemble micro-influencer teams, and campaigns execute as temporary micro-agencies — paid per project.

---

## The Problem

Influencer marketing is broken for small and mid-sized businesses.

- Agencies charge 40%+ margins and deliver generic campaigns
- Individual influencers have no structured way to work on real briefs
- India has a massive layer of knowledgeable, passionate people — finance students, food enthusiasts, tech creators — who have genuine niche expertise but zero entry point into the marketing economy

Follower count is currently the only currency. This platform creates a new one.

---

## How It Works

Three roles. One project. Everyone wins.

**Owner** — Posts a campaign brief with target audience, content category, minimum expectations, and incentive structure. Reviews pitches, selects the best one, confirms the team, and steps back.

**Knowledge Person (KP)** — The campaign architect. Picks up a brief, generates the campaign idea, assembles an influencer team, and serves as the single source of truth for the entire project. Competes against other KPs in a pitch round — selected on idea quality, not follower count or credentials.

**Influencer** — Works under KP direction. Gets matched to campaigns that fit their niche. Cannot contact the owner directly — reducing owner burden and keeping the KP as the single point of accountability.

---

## Core Flow
```
Owner posts brief
       ↓
Multiple KPs pitch (idea + team + committed metric)
       ↓
Owner selects best pitch
       ↓
KP finalizes team → one confirmation meeting with owner
       ↓
Group chat + deadlines auto-assigned
       ↓
Campaign executes
       ↓
Ratings updated · Payment released
```

---

## Key Features (MVP)

- Campaign brief creation with audience targeting
- KP pitch submission — idea, content angle, team lineup, committed metric
- Competitive pitch round — owner selects based on vision not credentials
- Team registration and deadline tracking on platform
- Auto-created group chat + VC per campaign
- Trust and rating system for all three roles
- Payment escrow — released on campaign completion

---

## Why This Is Different

Most influencer platforms are two-sided — brand meets influencer, done. This platform introduces the **Knowledge Person** role: a campaign strategist whose value is their niche knowledge and creative thinking, not their follower count. This unlocks an entirely new layer of talent that currently has no formal entry point into the creator economy.

Over time, every campaign generates **recipe data** — which team combinations, content angles, and brief types produce what results. This becomes a hyperlocal intelligence layer for Indian Tier 2/3 markets that no agency or platform currently has in structured form.
